Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use a small number of them to make Five Ways Pharmacy work and to process payments and bookings securely. This policy explains which we use and how to control them.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These are required for the site to function and cannot be switched off. They include cookies set when you sign in to an account, and security/fraud-prevention and session cookies set by Stripe (payments) and our scheduling provider Cal.com (bookings) when you use those features.

Functional

These remember choices you make — for example, dismissing the cookie notice, or keeping you signed in to your account.

Analytics

[If you add analytics such as Vercel Web Analytics or Google Analytics, describe it here and make sure it is only enabled with consent. If you do not use analytics, you can delete this section.]

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can usually set your browser to block them. Note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may stop parts of the site — such as payment or booking — from working.

Most browsers explain how to manage cookies in their help pages (for example, Chrome, Safari, Firefox and Edge all have cookie settings under privacy).

Contact

Questions about cookies? Contact us at pharmacy.frk99@nhs.net. See also our Privacy Policy.
