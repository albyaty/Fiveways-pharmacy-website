Cookie policy

This page explains the types of cookies Five Ways Pharmacy may use on the website, what they do, and how you can manage them through your browser settings.

Essential cookies only No advertising cookies Browser controls available

At a glance

  • Cookies help the website function properly
  • Advertising cookies are not used
  • You can manage cookies through your browser settings
Essential cookies

Needed for core website functions

  • These help core parts of the site work correctly
  • They can support basic security, accessibility, or session handling
  • Without them, some website features may not work as expected
Preference cookies

Remembering simple choices

  • These can remember practical choices such as closing a notice
  • They are used to improve your experience, not to build advertising profiles
  • If preference cookies are blocked, the site may still work but may be less convenient
What we do not use

No third-party advertising cookies

Five Ways Pharmacy does not use cookies for third-party advertising. If optional analytics or similar tools are introduced in future, this page should be updated first.

How do I manage cookies?

You can manage or delete cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers let you block some or all cookies.

What happens if I block cookies?

Some parts of the website may work less smoothly if essential or preference cookies are blocked.

Do you use advertising cookies?

No. Five Ways Pharmacy does not use advertising cookies.
