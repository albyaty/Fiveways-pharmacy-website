Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Five Ways Pharmacy uses a limited number of cookies to help the website work properly and remember basic preferences.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They may support security, page delivery, session handling, and other basic website functions.

Functional

These cookies can remember simple choices, such as whether you have dismissed a notice or other basic site preferences.

Analytics

Optional analytics cookies are not currently used on this website. If that changes in future, this page will be updated before those cookies are introduced.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can usually set your browser to block them. Blocking essential cookies may mean some parts of the website do not work as expected.

Most browsers explain how to manage cookies in their help pages, including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.

Questions about cookies? Contact us at pharmacy.frk99@nhs.net. See also our Privacy Policy.
