1. About our services

Five Ways Pharmacy provides a range of NHS and private pharmacy services, which may include:

  • NHS Pharmacy First consultations for common conditions;
  • NHS and private vaccinations (such as flu and COVID-19);
  • NHS contraception services;
  • stop smoking support and weight management;
  • dispensing and delivery of prescription medicines;
  • general health advice and over-the-counter sales;
  • online appointment booking and secure online payment.

The specific services available, and whether they are NHS-funded or private, are as shown on our website and may change from time to time.

3. Prescriptions & medicines

  • Prescription-only medicines are supplied only against a valid prescription and at the professional discretion of our pharmacist.

  • You are responsible for providing accurate patient details (including name, date of birth and any relevant health information).
  • Always read the label and the patient information leaflet, and use medicines as directed. Check expiry dates before use and do not use medicines beyond their expiry date.
  • Our pharmacist may need a further conversation, or a face-to-face assessment, before supplying certain medicines.

Your statutory rights under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 and the Consumer Rights Act 2015 are not affected. If something is wrong with your order, or you paid in error, contact us promptly at pharmacy.frk99@nhs.net and we will put it right, including a refund where appropriate. Refunds are made to your original payment method.
