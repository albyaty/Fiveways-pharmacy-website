Support areas

What can Weight Loss Support help with?

The consultation focuses on safe assessment, realistic planning, and supportive next steps based on your individual situation.

Goals and expectations

What we may cover

Your goals, the timeframe you are hoping for, and whether those expectations look realistic and safe.

How support may help

We can help you shape a more practical plan that fits your health background and lifestyle.

Important note

The consultation is about safe support and suitability, not guaranteed outcomes.

Lifestyle and routine support

What we may cover

Eating patterns, activity levels, routines, and practical changes that could support healthier progress.

How support may help

We can discuss realistic adjustments and identify where extra structure or monitoring may be useful.

Important note

Advice is tailored to the information you provide and the overall safety picture.

Suitability and clinical safety

What we may cover

Relevant medical history, current medicines, and whether further checks are needed before any treatment pathway is considered.

How support may help

We can explain what information matters most and why some options may be suitable for some patients but not others.

Important note

Suitability depends on clinical assessment, and treatment supply is never guaranteed.

Next-step planning

What we may cover

What happens after the first consultation, what further information may be needed, and when another service may be more appropriate.

How support may help

We’ll aim to leave you with a clear next step, whether that is pharmacy support, monitoring, lifestyle focus, or signposting.

Important note

If the service is not suitable, we may advise you to speak to your GP or another healthcare service instead.

Suitability

Who it is for

Weight Loss Support may suit adults who want professional guidance, a clearer understanding of suitability, or a safer plan for approaching weight management with pharmacy support.

The service is not suitable for everyone, and the pharmacy team will assess whether pharmacy-led support is appropriate or whether another healthcare route would be safer.

Be prepared

What to expect or what we may ask

  • Your height, weight, BMI, and weight loss goals where relevant
  • Current medicines, medical history, and allergies
  • Relevant lifestyle information such as routine, diet, and activity
  • Any previous support or treatment you have already tried
  • Other health information that may affect suitability or next-step advice

Questions

Weight Loss Support FAQs

Is the first consultation free?

Yes. The first consultation is free and is designed to help you understand suitability, safe support options, and what the next step may look like.

Can I have the consultation by telephone or Zoom?

Yes, telephone or Zoom appointments may be available. We can confirm the consultation format when you contact the pharmacy.

Will treatment be suitable for everyone?

No. Suitability depends on clinical assessment, your medical history, current medicines, and other relevant health information.

What information may I need to provide?

We may ask about your height, weight, BMI, health background, current medicines, lifestyle, and weight loss goals so we can assess the safest next step.

Are results guaranteed?

No. Results are never guaranteed, and any support plan should be based on safe, realistic expectations rather than promises.

Important guidance

Weight loss support depends on clinical assessment and individual suitability, and treatment supply or outcomes cannot be guaranteed. If you have significant new symptoms, rapid unexplained weight change, or feel unwell, contact your GP or NHS 111 for medical advice.
