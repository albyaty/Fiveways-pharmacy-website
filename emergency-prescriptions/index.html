Emergency supply

If you have completely run out of a medicine that you are prescribed regularly through a repeat prescription, NHS 111 online may be able to arrange a limited emergency supply. This service is for urgent, one-off situations and is not a regular way to order repeat medication.

When this may help

One-off urgent supply for regular repeat medication

This route may help if you have completely run out of a medicine you are prescribed regularly through a repeat prescription, are away from home, or cannot get your usual repeat prescription in time.

When this may help
  • You have completely run out of regular repeat medication
  • You are away from home
  • You cannot get your repeat prescription in time
  • You need a limited urgent supply until your usual repeat prescription is arranged
Important to know

NHS checks still apply

  • NHS 111 online will ask questions and suggest the best way to get help
  • A request does not guarantee supply
  • The usual NHS prescription charge may apply unless you are exempt

Questions

Emergency supply FAQs

What is emergency supply?

It is a limited urgent supply of medication for one-off situations when you have completely run out of a medicine that is usually prescribed regularly through a repeat prescription.

When might this route help?

This route may help if you have run out of regular repeat medication, are away from home, or cannot get your usual repeat prescription in time.

How do I choose Five Ways Pharmacy in NHS 111 online?

When NHS 111 online asks which pharmacy should receive the request, choose Five Ways Pharmacy and use the address details shown on this page.

Is supply guaranteed?

No. Supply depends on NHS guidance, pharmacist checks, and medicine suitability.

Important guidance

This NHS route is for one-off urgent supply and is not a regular way to order repeat medication. If you need urgent medical help for symptoms, use NHS 111 or emergency services as appropriate.
