1. What data we collect

  • Identity & patient details — name, date of birth, and (where the medication is for someone else) the patient’s name.
  • Contact details — email address, phone number and delivery address.
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We do not see or store your full card number; we receive a confirmation, the amount, and limited details such as the cardholder name and the last 4 digits.
  • Health information — any details you provide when booking or using a pharmacy service, or relating to a prescription (see section 3).
  • Account information — if you create an account, your email and a securely hashed password (managed by our authentication provider).
  • Booking information — appointment details you provide when booking via our scheduling tool.
  • Technical data — basic information your browser sends (such as IP address) and cookies (see section 7).

2. How and why we use it, and our lawful bases

  • To provide pharmacy services, dispense and deliver prescriptions, and take payment — performance of a contract with you, and compliance with our legal and professional obligations as a pharmacy.
  • To manage appointments and contact you about your order or booking — performance of a contract and our legitimate interests in running the pharmacy.
  • To meet healthcare, record-keeping and regulatory duties — compliance with legal obligations.
  • To send you a receipt and service updates — performance of a contract.
  • To keep the website secure and prevent fraud — our legitimate interests.

3. Health information (special category data)

Information about your health is “special category” data and is given extra protection in law. Where you provide health information so that we can provide pharmacy services or supply medicines, we process it for the provision of health care by a regulated health professional, and/or with your explicit consent. Our pharmacists handle this information confidentially and in line with professional standards.

4. Who we share it with

We never sell your data. We share it only where necessary, with:

  • Stripe — to process payments securely.
  • Our scheduling provider (Cal.com) — to manage appointment bookings.
  • Our hosting and infrastructure providers (Vercel; Supabase for accounts; Resend for transactional email) — to run the website, store account data and send emails.
  • The NHS, prescribers and delivery couriers — where needed to fulfil a service or deliver your medication.
  • Regulators or authorities — where we are legally required to do so.

Some of these providers may process data outside the UK. Where they do, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as UK adequacy regulations or standard contractual clauses).

5. How long we keep it

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above and to meet legal, professional and pharmacy record-keeping requirements (for example, records relating to the supply of medicines are kept for the periods required by law). When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymise it.

6. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • ask us to correct inaccurate data;
  • ask us to erase data (where this does not conflict with our legal duties);
  • restrict or object to certain processing;
  • request a copy of certain data in a portable format;
  • withdraw consent where we rely on it.

To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below.
